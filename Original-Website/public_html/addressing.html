How the Address System Works

Addresses in general runs from West to East, and South to North starting in the Southwest corner of Limestone County and increasing as you go North and East.

Note:This system applies to all areas of Limestone County including all municipalities - except for portions of the City of Athens and the portions of Limestone County annexed into the City of Madison.

Distanced Based System Addressing

- Each 1000 addresses equals one (1) mile (5,280 feet)

-Each 100 addresses equals one-tenth (1/10) of a mile (528 feet)

Even/Odd Addressing

When addresses increase (example:33100 to 33600), even addresses are on the right side of the roadway and odd addresses are on the left.

When addresses decrease (example:36000 to 31000), even addresses are on the left side of the roadway and odd addresses are on the right.

Advantages of using our system

- It helps emergency responders find a location quickly.
- The address number will be permanent even if the road name changes
- Allows for future expansion such as multiple dwellings on a single lot, extensions of existing roads, and future creation of subdivisions and roads

Note:In order for us to address a structure we require there to be stakes up marking where the 4 corners of the structure will be.
